🏆 How It Works

Each league contains 15 teams. The top team in each league wins $1,000 + a VIP ticket into the Champions Cup with a $10,000 grand prize. 2nd place gets $500, and teams placing 3rd-5th earn a free league entry for next season.

🎟️ Entry & Discounts

The standard buy-in is $250. But early birds who sign up by January 31st get $25 off. Want more? Refer a friend and you’ll get an extra $10 credit toward future entries or fantasy merch!

📈 Why Join?

This isn’t your standard fantasy league. You’re entering an elite competition that rewards strategy, dedication, and advanced metrics. It's like playing the stock market of baseball—except the payout is real, and so is the thrill.
